1

Pre-Sale Preparation

  • Locate your HCD Certificate of Title
    This is the ownership document issued by the CA Dept. of Housing and Community Development. If lost, file Form HCD RT 480.4 (Application for Duplicate Certificate of Title).
  • Locate your current Registration Card
    If lost, file Form HCD RT 481.2 (Application for Duplicate Registration Card).
  • Check your Registration Decal
    Note if it starts with "L" (local tax) or "A" (in-lieu tax) — this determines which tax forms are needed at closing.
  • Verify title is clear of liens
    Pay off any existing loans or liens. Contact your lender for a lien release/payoff statement.
  • Confirm all owners listed on title are available to sign
    All registered owners must sign the Certificate of Title for transfer.
  • Contact Hacienda Estates management (Caritas Corporation)
    Inform them of your intent to sell. Ask about: buyer approval process, transfer fees, current lot rent, and any park rules for resale.

Safety & Compliance Requirements

  • Install/verify working smoke detectors
    Required by California law before transfer. Test all units and replace batteries.
  • Install/verify carbon monoxide detectors
    Required in all homes with gas appliances or attached garages.
  • Verify water heater is seismically braced
    California requires all water heaters to be properly strapped/braced. This is checked on the transfer form.
  • Complete property disclosure of known defects
    Disclose any known material defects, past repairs, water damage, pest issues, etc.
⚠️ Legal Requirement Failure to disclose known defects can result in legal liability after the sale. Be thorough and honest in your disclosure statement.
3

Required Documents for HCD Transfer

  • Certificate of Title (original, signed by all owners)
    This is the primary ownership document. Must be signed on the back by all listed owners.
  • Current Registration Card
    Shows the home is currently registered in the state system.
  • Multi-Purpose Transfer Form (HCD RT 476.6G)
    Documents the ownership transfer and includes safety compliance certifications (smoke/CO detectors, water heater bracing).
  • Bill of Sale
    Written contract signed by both buyer and seller with sale price, terms, and date.
  • Tax Documentation (one of the following):

    • If decal starts with "L": Tax Clearance Certificate from LA County Tax Collector
    • If decal starts with "A": Form HCD RT 476.4 (Certification of Retail Value and Purchase Price)
  • Lien release documentation (if applicable)
    If there was a loan on the home, the lender must provide a lien release letter.
ℹ️ Tip: Use a Mobile Home Escrow Company Consider using a licensed escrow company that specializes in manufactured home transactions. They handle all paperwork, verify tax status, coordinate with the park, and ensure funds are securely transferred. This significantly reduces risk of errors.
4

Park Approval Process (Hacienda Estates)

  • Buyer submits residency application to Caritas Corporation
    The park will conduct a credit check and background check on the prospective buyer.
  • Park management approves buyer for residency
    This is required before the sale can be finalized. The park has the right to reject applicants.
  • Pay any park transfer fees
    Ask management about any fees associated with the transfer of space occupancy.
  • Buyer signs new lot rental agreement with park
    The buyer will enter into a new lease agreement for Space 194 directly with Caritas Corporation.
⚠️ Important: Timing Do NOT submit HCD transfer paperwork until the park has approved the buyer. If the park rejects the buyer, you cannot complete the sale for that space.
5

Closing & Title Transfer

  • Execute Bill of Sale with buyer
    Both parties sign. Include sale price, payment terms, and closing date.
  • Open escrow (recommended)
    Funds are held securely until all conditions are met and paperwork is filed.
  • Obtain Tax Clearance Certificate
    From LA County Tax Collector (if decal starts with "L"). Verify current property tax status.
  • Complete & sign HCD Multi-Purpose Transfer Form (RT 476.6G)
    Both buyer and seller must sign. Certifies safety compliance.
  • Submit all documents + fees to HCD
    Mail to: Registration and Titling, P.O. Box 277820, Sacramento, CA 95827-7820. Include filing fees (verify current amount with HCD).
  • Hand over keys and close escrow
    Once HCD confirms receipt and all conditions are met, funds are released and keys are transferred.
  • Cancel utilities in your name
    Transfer or cancel electric, gas, water, internet, and any other services.
✅ Congratulations! Once the HCD processes the transfer and the buyer receives their new Certificate of Title, the sale is officially complete.
